About Local Dish

WHAT IS THE LOCAL DISH?

The Local Dish is an Italian based pizzeria which was established in the year 1995. The main restaurant locations of Local Dish are originally located in the home of Italy, but due to recent sales, we've decided to take the company overseas to further distribute our brand. As this is our first restaurant located overseas, we've decided on a way to reach new customers fairly quickly. Creating a website to promote Local Dish was what was decided upon within the company's administration board.


Here at Local Dish, we aim to provide some of the best quality meals we can offer to our wonderful customers.
